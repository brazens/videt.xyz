Border style

This utility class specifies the border styles.

Class Properties
border-none border-style: none;
border-dotted border-style: dotted;
border-dashed border-style: dashed;
border-solid border-style: solid;
border-double border-style: double;
border-groove border-style: groove;
border-ridge border-style: ridge;
border-inset border-style: inset;
border-outset border-style: outset;

use

Describes the style of the border.

None

use it by including "border-none" in your class.
Does not display borders.

You may delay, but time will not.

<p class="border-none ... "> You may delay, but time will not. </p>

Dotted

use it by including "border-dotted" in your class.
Draw the border with multiple round dots.

We give advice, but we cannot give conduct.

<p class="border-dotted ... "> We give advice, but we cannot give conduct. </p>

Dashed

use it by including "border-dashed" in your class.
Draw borders with multiple rectangles.

A Penny Saved is a Penny Earned.

<p class="border-dashed ... "> A Penny Saved is a Penny Earned. </p>

Solid

use it by including "border-solid" in your class.
Draw the border with a single straight line.

Never ruin an apology with an excuse.

<p class="border-solid ... "> Never ruin an apology with an excuse. </p>

Double

use it by including "border-double" in your class.
Draw the border with two parallel straight lines.

We are all born ignorant, but one must work hard to remain stupid.

<p class="border-double ... "> We are all born ignorant, but one must work hard to remain stupid. </p>

Groove

use it by including "border-groove" in your class.
Draw borders as if they were recessed. The opposite of ridge.

Well done is better than well said.

<p class="border-groove ... "> Well done is better than well said. </p>

Ridge

use it by including "border-ridge" in your class.
Draw the border as if it were sticking out. The opposite of groove.

There was never a bad peace or a good war.

<p class="border-ridge ... "> There was never a bad peace or a good war. </p>

Inset

use it by including "border-inset" in your class.
Draws the border as if the element was dug. The opposite of outset.

Love your Enemies, for they tell you your Faults.

<p class="border-inset ... "> Love your Enemies, for they tell you your Faults. </p>

Outset

use it by including "border-outset" in your class.
Draws the border as if the element was dug. The opposite of outset.

Never leave till tomorrow that which you can do today.

<p class="border-outset ... "> Never leave till tomorrow that which you can do today. </p>

All examples are quotes from Benjamin Franklin
